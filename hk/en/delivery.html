Hong Kong

Below are the basic shipping methods and fees: 

  1. Ta-Q-Bin (Local residential, commercial and industrial district): HK$25 per order under 3 kg.  HK$35 for orders between 3 to 5 kg.Tracking number will be available.
  2. Local courier (Limited to commercial and industrial district and order below 2kg): HK$13 per order. Tracking number not available.
  3. Cool Ta-Q-Bin (Recommended for Fresh Face Mask and Meltable products within Hong Kong only): HK$40 per order. Tracking number will be available.
  4. Domestic Hong Kong Post (Outlying islands and remote area within Hong Kong): HK$35 per order. Tracking number will be available.

Mainland China

Below are the basic shipping methods and fees (Customs clearance time is something we do not have fully control. Duration of shipping maybe delay due to customs clearance situation):

  1. Courier service (All area within Mainland China) 
    • First 0.5kg: HK$34
    • First 1kg: HK$44
    • Each additional kg: HK$44
  2. Hong Kong EC-Post* (Limited to following area and order below 2kg)
    • Guangdong Province (2kg or less): HK$59
    • Outside Guangdong Province (2kg or less): HK$69

*Customs duties would not be included in your order total. Additional duties might be applied upon delivery with EMS. Delivery areas include: Guangdong, Beijing, Shanghai, Fujian, Sichuan, Chongqing, Zhejiang, Shandong and Tianjin.
