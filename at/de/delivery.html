Delivery Information
Placing an order? We can’t wait to unite you with your items! Please bear in mind, however, that delivery estimates are calculated from the time of despatch, rather than from the moment you place your order. As each product is carefully picked and packed by hand, these estimates can vary, particularly during busy times of the year. So if your order isn’t despatched straight away, don’t panic! We’re on it.

Shipping Rates & Times

As we use a Courier service to safely deliver all of our orders, we are unable to send to PO Boxes or Private Bags. Please ensure you provide a full delivery address when ordering.
Just so you know, these delivery times apply to all orders placed before 1pm. For all orders placed after 1pm, the following day will be considered the first working day. Although we do our very best to ensure all orders placed are picked, packed and dispatched on the day they are ordered, there are times when this may not be possible. Even we need sleep!

All orders will be delivered by NZ Couriers and will require a signature, so please make sure there will be someone at home to sign for it.

Otherwise a card will be left and your parcel will need to be redelivered or collected from your local NZ couriers depot or agency. Please note NZ Couriers will only deliver from Monday to Friday. If your parcel contains any fresh face masks we will not send it out on the Friday.

Rural deliveries sometimes take a day or two longer as the parcel will be passed on to your local rural delivery driver. Please allow for this when ordering goods for urgent delivery.

Track Your Parcel

To track your parcel please contact us via email on mailorder@lushnz.com for the consignment number and estimated delivery time.

International Orders

Unfortunately we can no longer ship internationally. Please refer to www.lush.com to find a LUSH online store that will ship to your location.
