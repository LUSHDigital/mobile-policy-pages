Full Version

This statement discloses the privacy practices and policies for the Lush application. If you have any questions about these practices and policies, please email us at enquiries@lush.com.au.

Your personal data will be used for processing and invoicing your order and when dealing with your enquiries and, if you opt in, to send you information about Lush and its products. This may involve processing sensitive personal data about your health that you give to us.

If you would like to be kept up to date with all the new exciting Lush news, you can opt in for our newsletter and Lush Times! All you have to do sign-up via our app, website or customer care team and there you have it; you can then confirm that your e-mail address is correct and is the same address you have used to order with.

If you do not want to receive Lush Times or Lush emails anymore please e-mail enquiries@lush.com.au or contact us on 1300 587 428. If you would like to change your personal data on our database or you want a copy of the data we hold on file please e-mail enquiries@lush.com.au or call us on 1300 587 428.

We won’t share your information with our third party providers and group companies. We treat your data as strictly confidential!

Website Cookies

Our app uses a few main cookies;

We keep a session cookie which is a small file on our server allowing us to distinguish you from other users, so that we can keep a virtual "basket" of the products you add to your shopping cart.

We also use Google Analytics, which sets a small cookie file on your device. This cookie stores no personal information about you and will keep a track of how you use our app. We use this anonymous information to help us further improve the shopping experience on our app.

Some of the pages on the app use content embedded from another service, for example YouTube, you maybe sent cookies from these websites. We don’t actually control these cookies and we would recommend that you visit these third parties for more information about their cookies:

  • Adyen (Our payment provider)

We don’t sell the information collected by cookies, nor do we disclose the information to third parties, except where required by law (for example to government bodies and law enforcement agencies). We treat your information as sensitive and confidential.

If you continue without changing your settings, we’ll assume you are happy to accept all cookies on the Lush app.

If cookies aren’t enabled on your device, it will mean that your shopping experience on our website will be limited to browsing and researching. Unfortunately you will not be able to add products to your basket and buy them.
