Delivery Information
Placing an order? We can’t wait to unite you with your items! Please bear in mind, however, that delivery estimates are calculated from the time of despatch, rather than from the moment you place your order. As each product is carefully picked and packed by hand, these estimates can vary, particularly during busy times of the year. So if your order isn’t despatched straight away, don’t panic! We’re on it.
The majority of orders placed are processed and dispatched the next working day. Please note that credit card payments made after 6pm can take up to 48 hours to process.

Shipping Rates & Times

Shipping charges are fixed for orders throughout Australia.
All shipping estimates are from when payment is received by LUSH. Estimates are based on Australia Post shipping times.

Please note Australia Post only deliver from Monday to Friday.
