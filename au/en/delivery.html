Current Shipping Times UPDATED: 23rd April 2020

While our physical stores are currently closed, our online store is open! During this time, you can also enjoy Free Standard Shipping on any order over $95.00

 

With stores being closed, we are seeing increases in online orders at the moment, and due to the current COVID-19 situation, are taking extra steps and precautions to ensure the safety of our Fulfilment Team and our customers. This means we are a little slower than normal and at this stage, most orders are being dispatched 3-4 business days after they are placed. 

 

Due to COVID-19, there are also significant delays across the Australia Post network, and your parcel will take longer than regular delivery estimates to reach you.

 

As a result of these delays, your order may take 10-15 business days to reach you.  

 

We can’t wait to get your Lush goodies to you to help make this time easier, and thank you so much for your patience and understanding in these exceptional circumstances. If you have any further concerns or questions about your order, please do not hesitate to get in touch with our Customer Care Team.

 

 

 

More Information About Deliveries: 

Placing an order? We can’t wait to unite you with your items! Please bear in mind, however, that delivery estimates are calculated from the time of dispatch, rather than from the moment you place your order. As each product is carefully picked and packed by hand, these estimates can vary, particularly during busy times of the year. So if your order isn’t dispatched straight away, don’t panic! We’re on it.

 

The majority of orders placed in off-peak times are processed and dispatched the next working day. Please note that credit card payments made after 6pm can take up to 48 hours to process.
